That would be it for the "gaming" section, let's move onto something more interesting and important
As you might know, I'm playing through Cyberpunk 2077, and that game basically tells us what will the world look like if we keep letting the big corpos rule us. We have to keep decentralizing the internet so that no Meta, no Google, no Microsoft can have full power over it. How do you decentralize it? Well, you host a game server on your own hardware instead of using Google services. You can use a Linux distro, instead of running a Windows machine. You can create some forums, instead of relying on Facebook groups.
Ok, the point isn't for you to ditch 100% of those services, but the point is to limit how many of them you use, and try switching to alternatives if you can.
We also have to keep fighting for our privacy. No company or government should control us 24/7 or know everything about us. We should have the right to encrypt our messages without the ability of the government to know the message content. One can say "think of the children!", but think about it. Do you really want your kid to be observed by a government or some company 24/7? Should the kid really have no privacy just to "make him safe"?
